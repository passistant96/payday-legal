Privacy Policy

Effective: 24 February 2026

Overview

PayDay is a shift tracking and pay estimation app built by Evans Studio. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data We Collect

Short version: your data stays on your device.

PayDay stores all your shift data, job information, and settings locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal data.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact details
  • We do not collect your shift times, pay rates, or earnings data
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track your usage with analytics
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat is used to manage in-app subscriptions. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes the transaction through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase data from Apple. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple processes all payments through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed. We have no ability to recover deleted data as we never had access to it.

Children's Privacy

PayDay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@evans-studio.co.uk.
