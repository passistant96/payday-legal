Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 February 2026

PayDay ("the App") is developed by Evans Studio. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

PayDay stores all your data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Shift times and dates you enter
  • Job names and hourly rates
  • Settings and preferences (pay period, deductions, tax region)

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Your shift data never leaves your device.

Analytics and Tracking

PayDay does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, location, or behaviour.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you delete the App, your data is permanently removed. There is no cloud backup unless you use iCloud device backups provided by Apple.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
