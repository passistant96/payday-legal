What the App Does

PayDay is a shift tracking tool that estimates your earnings and take-home pay based on information you provide. It is designed for hourly and shift workers who want a quick view of what they will earn.

Estimates, Not Exact Figures

All pay calculations, tax deductions, National Insurance, student loan, and pension figures shown in the App are estimates only. They are based on standard UK tax codes (1257L) and published HMRC rates for the 2025/26 tax year. They may not reflect your actual deductions.

The App is not a payroll system, tax calculator, or financial advice tool. For accurate figures, refer to your payslip or contact HMRC directly.

Evans Studio accepts no liability for decisions made based on figures shown in the App.
