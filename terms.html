What PayDay Does

PayDay is a shift tracking tool that helps hourly and shift workers estimate their earnings and take-home pay. You log your shifts and the app calculates gross pay and estimated deductions.

We do not guarantee the accuracy of any calculation. Your actual take-home pay may differ based on your specific tax code, employment circumstances, benefits, and other factors.
