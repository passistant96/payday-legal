Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a shift?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Set your start time, end time, and the app will calculate your estimated pay based on your hourly rate.

How accurate are the tax estimates?

PayDay uses current UK tax bands and National Insurance thresholds to provide estimates. These are approximations only and should not be treated as financial advice. For precise figures, check with HMRC or a qualified accountant.

Where is my data stored?

All your shift data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or shift data on external servers.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any active Pro subscription linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select PayDay to manage or cancel.
